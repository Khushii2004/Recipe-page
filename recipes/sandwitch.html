Turkey and Avocado Sandwich

    Ingredients
  • 2 slices of whole-grain or sourdough bread (or your favorite type)
  • 4-6 slices of turkey breast (deli or cooked)
  • 1 tomato, sliced
  • ripe avocado
  • Leafy lettuce or spinach
    Instructions
  • To make a delicious turkey and avocado sandwich, start by toasting two slices of your favorite bread with a bit of butter or olive oil if desired. Mash a ripe avocado with a pinch of salt and pepper, and spread it evenly on one slice of the toasted bread. Layer with 4-6 slices of turkey breast, add sliced tomato, and a few leaves of lettuce or spinach. Optionally, spread mayonnaise and Dijon mustard on the other slice of bread before placing it on top of the sandwich. Cut in half if you like, and enjoy your fresh, flavorful sandwich!
